# Lead Magnet Strategy: Pro Graphics (Durban)

## OFFER 1: The "Hidden Cost Revealer"

**Headline:** *"The Durban Business Owner's Signage Cost Calculator: Know Exactly What You'll Pay Before You Quote"*

- **Type:** Reveal Their Problem (Diagnostic)
- **The Hook:** Most businesses have no idea what signage actually costs—they either overpay or get low-quality work because they don't understand pricing drivers. This tool gives them instant clarity.

### Deliverables

You will provide:

1. **Interactive Calculator:** (PDF or Web Form) showing price ranges for:
   - **Vehicle Wraps:** Spot graphics vs. Partial vs. Full wraps.
   - **Sign Boards:** Chromadek vs. ABS vs. Perspex.
   - **Window Coverage:** Contravision (50% vs. 80%).
2. **"Price Drivers" Sheet:** A breakdown of what causes prices to fluctuate.
3. **Budget Scenarios:** 3 distinct tiers (Starter, Professional, Premium) with real-world examples.

### Hormozi Framework Analysis: "Hidden Cost Revealer"

| Element | How It's Optimized |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Dream Outcome** | "Get fair pricing without being ripped off or embarrassed." |
| **Perceived Likelihood** | Showcasing actual Pro Graphics pricing tiers + competitor benchmarks. |
| **Time Delay** | **Instant** calculation—no waiting 24-48 hours for a manual quote callback. |
| **Effort & Sacrifice** | Near zero. Just check boxes; no design or technical knowledge required. |

## Email Sequence Integration (Automated Lead Nurturing)

*Goal: Move the lead from a diagnostic tool to a high-intent quote request.*

1. **Email 1: Value Delivery**
   - **Content:** The Calculator + "Here's what most Durban businesses get wrong about signage quotes."
2. **Email 2: Authority Building**
   - **Content:** "3 signs you got a bad quote (and how to fix it)."
3. **Email 3: Social Proof**
   - **Content:** Case study: "How [Local Business] saved R8,000 on their fleet branding."
4. **Email 4: Conversion (CTA)**
   - **Content:** "Get a real quote in 24 hours — no surprises."
   - **Action:** Direct link to Pro Graphics quote form.

## OFFER 2: "The Durban 10" Founding Fleet Offer

**Headline:** *"Be One of the Durban 10: Get 25% Off Your Next Vehicle Branding (Limited to the First 10 Customers)"*

- **Type:** Sample and Trial / Scarcity Offer
- **The Scarcity:** Strictly limited to 10 businesses.
- **The Urgency:** Must claim spot and pay deposit before [Insert Date, e.g., March 15th].
- **The Value:** A significant "Early Bird" discount to build a fresh 2026 portfolio of locally branded vehicles.

### Implementation Requirements

1. **Visual Scarcity:** A "Spots Remaining" tracker on the website (e.g., "Last 4 spots available!").
2. **Time Limit:** A clear expiration date on the offer banner.
3. **Low Friction Check-in:** A simple "Claim My Spot" button that captures the lead's name and vehicle type.

### Hormozi Framework Analysis: "The Durban 10"

| Element | How It's Optimized |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Dream Outcome** | High-end vehicle branding at a "founding member" price. |
| **Perceived Likelihood** | "First 10" makes it feel exclusive and high-priority. |
| **Time Delay** | Immediate confirmation of spot reservation. |
| **Effort & Sacrifice** | Requires a quick decision (Scarcity) but saves thousands in Rands. |
