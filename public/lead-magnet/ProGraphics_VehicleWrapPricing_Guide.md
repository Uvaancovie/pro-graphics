# Pro Graphics Vehicle Wrap Pricing Estimator (Durban 2026)

Use this quick guide to estimate your vehicle wrap budget before requesting a final quote.

## 1) Estimate vinyl length needed

Planning formula:

`vehicle length × 3 + extra (1.5m to 3m)`

The extra covers bumpers, overlap, and installation margin.

### Example

- Vehicle length: 4.95m
- Base: 4.95 × 3 = 14.85m
- Extra margin: +3m
- Estimated vinyl needed: 17.85m (plan around 18m roll)

## 2) Choose your vehicle class

| Vehicle class | Typical vinyl length |
|---|---|
| Small hatch/sedan | 15m–17m |
| Mid-size sedan/coupe | 18m–20m |
| Full-size sedan/small SUV | 20m–23m |
| Large SUV/bakkie/minivan | 23m–30m |

## 3) Pick coverage level

- Full wrap
- Partial wrap
- Commercial graphics

## 4) Pick film category

- Standard gloss/matte
- Premium color film
- Specialty finish (chrome, color-shift, textured)

## 5) Build your budget band

Use this planning model:

`Estimated cost = (Material tier × Size tier) + Labor complexity + Prep contingency`

Add a 10–20% contingency for:

- paint/trim prep
- difficult curves and bumpers
- timeline constraints

These are budget estimates only. Final pricing depends on exact model, finish, and install scope.

## 7) Quote checklist (send this to get accurate pricing)

Provide:

- vehicle make/model/year
- photos (front, back, both sides, roof if possible)
- wrap type (full, partial, graphics)
- color/finish preference
- preferred installation timeline

## 8) Before you book

- Ask what film brand is used
- Confirm warranty terms
- Confirm prep process and expected downtime
- Ask about aftercare guidance
